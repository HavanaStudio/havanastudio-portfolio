Leather Accessories Campaign

Concept

A campaign built around heritage, craft and quiet permanence. The object as heirloom — not as product.

Campaign Construction

Building the universe

Environment

The cortijo

A stone farmhouse in Extremadura. Warm afternoon light through old wood. A space that speaks of time, not trend.

+

Character

The woman

Still. Present. She does not pose with the bag — she simply exists beside it, as if it has always been hers.

+

Product

The bag

Pentagonal geometry in burgundy leather. Treated not as accessory but as the centrepiece of the visual narrative.

= Final Result

All elements integrate into a single visual language.

The environment, the woman and the object become one. The campaign does not sell a bag — it communicates what kind of person owns one.
